Frequently Asked Questions — Pest Control in Delhi

How much does pest control cost in Delhi?

Pest control costs in Delhi depend on property size and pest type. A 1BHK cockroach treatment starts from ₹800–₹1,200. Rodent control for a 2BHK begins at ₹1,500. We provide a free inspection and written quote before starting — no hidden charges.

Why do rodent problems increase in Delhi during winter?

As temperatures drop in November–January, rodents like rats and mice seek warmth and food inside homes, offices and warehouses. Delhi's dense neighbourhoods with older construction provide easy entry points. Early-winter rodent-proofing treatments are highly recommended.

Do you cover Gurgaon and Noida as well as Delhi?

Yes. We serve all Delhi NCR areas including Gurgaon (Gurugram), Noida, Greater Noida, Faridabad and Ghaziabad. Same-day service is available across most NCR locations when you call before noon.

Is pest control safe for children and pets in Delhi homes?

Absolutely. We use WHO and CIB&RC approved chemicals that are safe once dry. Our technicians follow strict application protocols and advise you to vacate the treated area for 2–4 hours. We always provide complete safety instructions.

How often should I do pest control in my Delhi home?

For most Delhi homes, a quarterly AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) is recommended. Monsoon and pre-winter treatments are especially important. Restaurants and commercial premises benefit from monthly or bi-monthly service for ongoing compliance.
